The Best Movies According to Moaz

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Extraction

I enjoy "Extraction" because of its intense action scenes, gripping storyline, and impressive performances, particularly by Chris Hemsworth.

john wick

I like the "John Wick" movies for their stylish action sequences, compelling world-building, and Keanu Reeves' excellent performance.

All the Bright Places

I like "All the Bright Places" for its heartfelt storytelling, strong performances by the cast, and its sensitive exploration of mental health issues.
